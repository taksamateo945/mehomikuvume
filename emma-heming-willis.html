Early Life and Childhood

Emma Heming Willis was born Emma Frances Heming to a British father and an Indo-Guyanese mother, Zorina Heming.

She was born on June 18, 1978, in Malta. Her age is currently 45 years old. When Emma was a young child, her parents got divorced, and she and her mother moved to London before making their way to Southern California, where they eventually settled.

Emma was raised by her mother in North London and California. Likewise, she holds three nationalities British, Maltese, and American. She is of Multiracial ethnic background. According to her birthdate, she was born under the sun sign Gemini.

Modeling Career

In the early 1990s, Emma Heming began her modeling career after winning “The British Elle Supermodel” competition. She was a model for the London agency Lorraine Ashton early in her career.

She has been involved in the modeling business ever since. Emma has worked as a spokesmodel for the Canadian lingerie retailer La Senza. She has been showcased in commercials for a variety of companies, including Gap, Escada, Intimissimi, Palmers, Dior Bronze, and Redken.

In addition to these, Heming has appeared on the covers of numerous publications, including “W Magazine,” “Town and Country,” “Shape,” and “US Glamour.” Herve Leger, Paco Rabanne, John Galliano, Christian Dior, Maska, Chanel, Thierry Mugler, Emanuel Ungaro, Valentino, Victoria’s Secret, and Ralph Lauren are just a few of the designers and companies for which she has walked the runways.

Acting Career

Emma Heming’s first acting role came in the American movie “Perfume” in 2001, which served as her debut performance. She then appeared in an episode of the TV show “Entourage” in 2006. She portrayed Donna in the American neo-noir psychological thriller movie “Perfect Stranger” the following year.

Heming also made an appearance in Tom Brady’s satirical comedy “The Comebacks” that same year. She was chosen to star in “Red 2,” an action comedy and the follow-up to the 2010 film Red.
