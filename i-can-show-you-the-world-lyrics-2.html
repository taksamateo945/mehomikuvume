I Can Show You The World Lyrics

 Posted on July 15, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  515 words  |  Martina Birk

I Can Show You The World by Lea Salonga & Brad Kane is a romantic duet that was featured in the 1992 animated film Aladdin.

The song is sung by Aladdin and Jasmine, as they fly on a magic carpet and explore the wonders of the world. The song is composed by Alan Menken, with lyrics by Tim Rice.

Facts About I Can Show You The World Lyrics

FactDetails
SongwritersAlan Menken and Tim Rice
Released1992
AlbumAladdin: Original Motion Picture Soundtrack
GenrePop, ballad
Length2:40
Chart performance#1 on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart for a week
CertifiedGold by the RIAA
InfluencesThe song is said to be influenced by the 1940 film “The Thief of Bagdad”.
ThemesThe song explores themes of love, adventure, and freedom.
LyricsThe lyrics are about Aladdin and Jasmine’s newfound love and their desire to explore the world together. The song is full of imagery of flying carpets, magic lamps, and faraway lands.
Music videoThe music video for “I Can Show You The World” was directed by Hendel Butoy and features Lea Salonga and Brad Kanee performing the song on a magic carpet. The video was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Music Video.
Legacy“I Can Show You The World” is one of the most popular songs from Disney’s animated films and has been covered by many artists, including Peabo Bryson and Regina Belle, Christina Aguilera, and Josh Groban
