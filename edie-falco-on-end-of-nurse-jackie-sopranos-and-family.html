Edie Falco on end of “Nurse Jackie,” “Sopranos” and family – CBS News

Showtime’s “Nurse Jackie” injects dark humor into the serious issue of prescription drug addiction for its seventh and final season. Edie Falco plays Jackie Peyton, a lying, cheating, pill-popping ER nurse whose addiction is finally exposed. Falco joins “CBS This Morning” for a preview of Sunday’s episode and to discuss the ending of the show.
