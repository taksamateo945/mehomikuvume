Whitney Cummings Biography

Whitney Cummings is best known as Comedian, actress, writer, producer, director, podcaster who has an estimated Net Worth of $30 Million. Comedian and actress who gained fame as a cast member on Punk'd. She created and starred in the NBC sitcom Whitney, which premiered in 2012, and co-created the CBS sitcom, 2 Broke Girls. She was born on September 4,1982, Washington, DC. She appeared in the 2008 film Made of Honor and released her Comedy Central standup special, Whitney Cummings: Money Shot, in 2010. She attended the University of North Carolina, transferred to the University of Pennsylvania, and graduated magna cum laude in 2004 with degrees in communications and film. She has appeared on The Tonight Show with TV show host Conan O'Brien. She began dating Peter Berg in 2013. Her mother is Patti Cummings and her brother is Richard Cummings Jr. Her zodiac sign is Virgo
