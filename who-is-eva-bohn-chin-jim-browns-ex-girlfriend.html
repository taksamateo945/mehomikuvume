Eva Bohn-Chin is the ex-girlfriend of Jim Brown, the late American football fullback, civil rights activist, and actor. She was born on 29th December 1946 and is now 76 years old.

Besides the massive fame she gained through her relationship with the late NFL star, Eva Bohn-Chin had a recognized career as a fashion model and was considerably recognized in the modeling industry. What’s more, Eva did not just get public attention for dating Jim, but significantly for the fact that their relationship was a distressed one.

Besides the massive recognition and fame he commanded, thanks to his successful professional life, Jim Brown was notorious for the relationships he had with multiple women. While a few of them were peaceful, many others, including his relationship with Eva, were filled with scandals, toxicity, allegations and counter-allegations.

What Happened Between Eva Bohn-Chin and Jim Brown?

Eva and Jim Brown started dating in 1968, although it is not known exactly where and when they met for the first time. At this time, she was 22 years old while Jim was 32. It is, however, known that Jim’s first wife Sue Brown had just sued for a divorce citing “gross neglect.” They were still living together but processing their divorce case when he met Eva, and they started a relationship.

At first, their relationship blossomed and it seemed like a happily ever kind of love. They were seen together at events and other public ceremonies. Eva was very supportive of Jim’s career and was usually spotted cheering him in the field. However, this was not after a few months later when news began to make rounds that their relationship was getting toxic.

In the same year they started dating, precisely on the 9th of June, one of their neighbors in Hollywood overheard an argument in Jim’s apartment and immediately dialed 911. The cops swiftly responded to the call and upon their arrival, they found Eva semi-conscious beneath the balcony of Jim’s West Hollywood second-floor apartment. They transported her to USC County Medical Center for prompt medical attention.

She was later transferred to Cedars of Lebanon Hospital for further care and evaluation. The actual circumstance or what they were arguing about that led to this incident was not disclosed but it portrayed that the duo had been facing challenges in their relationship even before that particular day. Meanwhile, Brown was arrested and faced a series of allegations including an accusation of throwing her off the balcony.

He was charged with multiple offenses including felony battery against a law enforcement officer, assault with the intention to cause murder, and obstructing the legal process. He first got released on a $12,500 bail set and his charges were later changed to resisting a deputy with a fine of $300. This was after Eva dropped his charge of assault with intent to commit murder. The then 22-year-old would reveal that she fell from the balcony when the cops arrived.

Where Is Jim Brown’s Ex-girlfriend Now?

Being an elegant woman with a captivating blend of beauty inherited from her Jamaican father and German mother, Bohn-Chin in her own right was able to make a name for herself in the American modeling scene. However, it won’t be a surprise that the younger generation of models may not be familiar with her because she is now retired, as she is 76 years old.

Eva Bohn-Chin’s name resonates well with the older generation of models and significantly with millions of American pro football fans, thanks to her publicized relationship with Jim Brown. However, the former celebrity partner appears to be living entirely away from the limelight ever since. She does not have an active presence on any social media platform and is not seen at any public events.

According to limited information, she currently lives in California, United States of America, though what she is precisely up to is not public knowledge. Indeed, her toxic relationship with Jim Brown was precisely what gave her the height of public recognition she sustains to date, even more than other women the NFL star dated.

Her Ex, Jim Brown, was Notorious For Scandalous Relationships and Affairs with Women

Eva Bohn-Chin’s ex-boyfriend, Jim Brown is a famous American football fullback, civil rights activist, and actor who played for the Cleveland Browns in the NFL from 1957 through 1965. However, notable is the fact that he had a dark side when it comes to domestic violence. “Jim Brown Did Great Things; He Also Beat Women” was once a popular headline for many media outlets and news sites.

Jim was reportedly arrested at least seven times for assault mainly against women but, he never served any significant jail sentence. In most of the cases, the women involved refused to press charges even after calling the police, just like in Eva’s case. Yet, his stories of violence, especially with women, cannot be ignored. Besides Eva Bohn-Chin, Jim Brown encountered a series of other scandalous affairs with women.

In 1965, he was arrested in his hotel room for assault and battery against an 18-year-old Brenda Ayres. In 1975, he was convicted of misdemeanor battery for beating and choking his golfing partner, Frank Snow, In 1985, he was charged with raping a 33-year-old woman. However, as the story goes, he was acquitted of all of the charges.

These are only but a few of his numerous rape and assault cases. In fact, according to several witnesses and victims who were interviewed for Secrets of Playboy, the 2022 documentary series, Jim Brown brutally raped and assaulted numerous women and young girls at the Playboy Mansion from the late 1970s into the 1990s.

Jim Brown passed away at the age of 87 on 20th May 2023 at his home in Los Angeles, California. He died of natural causes with his wife at his side. Notably, his recent death was the reason his ex-girlfriend Eva surfaced in the limelight despite the fact that their relationship happened many decades ago and had almost been forgotten by the internet.
