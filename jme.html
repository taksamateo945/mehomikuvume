JME Age & Biography

JME, who was born on May 4, 1985, and is currently 37 years old in 2023. He was raised in a middle-class household in Hackney, East London, and was born in Tottenham, North London, England. He is of British nationality and follows the Christian faith. He finished his primary and secondary education at St. Paul’s School and Winchmore School in Winchmore Hill, Enfield.

Following that, he enrolled in the University of Greenwich, where he received a first-class honors degree in 3D Digital Design. Since his childhood, he has been fascinated by technology and began making mobile phone ringtones. Later, he turned his attention to music and began producing raps with his brother. Taurus is his astrological sign.

Career

JME began his career making mobile phone ringtones in 2006. In 2008, he published his debut album, “Famous?” which included 15 songs.

In October 2010, he released another album named “Blam!”, with the singles “Over Me”, “Sidetracked”, and “CD is Dead”. He published a compilation album titled “History:” in February 2011, which scored 162 ranks in the “UK Albums Chart”.

In May 2015, he released his third studio album, “Integrity>,” which debuted at number twelve on the UK Albums Chart. After almost four years on 29 November 2019, he released his fourth studio album titled “Grime MC” garnering 8/10 in the music and fashion magazine “Clash” and was also highly liked by the American online music newspaper “Pitchfork”.

Following that, from 2020 to 2021, he worked with a number of well-known rappers and musicians, including “Skepta,” “Jammer,” “Shorty,” “Frisco,” and others.

He released a joint album titled “Norf Face” with “Capo Lee,” “Frisco,” and “Shorty” in March 2021.
