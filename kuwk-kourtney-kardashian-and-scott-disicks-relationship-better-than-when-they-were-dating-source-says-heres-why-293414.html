 Posted on May 25, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  322 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Kourtney Kardashian and Scott Disick may no longer be a couple but that does not mean they don’t have a harmonious relationship. In fact, it seems like they are better than ever now, just co-parenting and being friends.

One insider report claims Scott and Kourt are in a very good place nowadays and wouldn’t have it any other way.

It’s been four years since they broke up already but the exes who share three kids together are closer than when they used to date, apparently.

The fans know that they were together for almost a decade but they always used to have problems, splitting and getting back together multiple times until they finally ended it for good.

Now, they have both moved on from what they once had and simply reinvented the type of relationship they have since they are still pretty much constantly in each other’s lives.

One source tells HollyWoodLife that ‘It’s very important for the Kardashians for Scott to be happy and they see how happy Sofia makes him. Kris [Jenner] has always told her daughters how important it is to get to a good place with their children’s fathers, which is why Kourtney and Scott are in such a great place. Their relationship is better now than it was when they were together and he’s very close to the whole family still.’

‘He will always be family to them and Kendall and Kylie have known Sofia for years. They know she’s a good person,’ the insider went on to tell the news outlet.

While Kourtney is yet to find love of her own, Scott and Sofia are doing great despite the 15 years age gap that bothered so many people at first including Kourtney.

But now, the insider claims she just sees how much the kids love her and doesn’t care anymore.
