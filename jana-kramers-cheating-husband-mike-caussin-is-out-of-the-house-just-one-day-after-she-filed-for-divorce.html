JANA Kramer’s cheating husband Mike Caussin is “out of the house” just one day after she filed for divorce.

The actress, 37, announced they were going their separate ways earlier this week on Instagram after six years of marriage.

And now, according to US weekly, the split “has already altered their home life”.

The former NFL player, 33, is already “out of the house” that he once shared with his wife and two kids Jolie Rae, five, and Jace Joseph, two.

Since then Jana has had time to reflect over what’s happened and shared a number of cryptic posts on her Instagram Story.

One said: “Sometimes we don’t need to understand. We just need to believe.”

Another stated: “Trust in him. Every step is a step of faith.”

A third read: “Not all storms come to disrupt your life. Some come to clear your path.”

After years of going behind his wife’s back, Jana decided to end their marriage, explaining to fans that she has “nothing else to give”.

She told her 2m followers on Instagram in a lengthy post: “It’s time. As I try to make sense of a reality I never wanted to believe could be possible again, those words have now become a reality.

“I’ve fought y’all. I’ve loved hard. I’ve forgiven. I’ve put the work in. I’ve given everything I have, and now I have nothing else to give. It’s time.”

She continued: “Please know that I still believe wholeheartedly in marriage, love, and rebuilding.

“I just can’t fight any longer. It’s time to heal.

“Thank you for all the love, heard, and support, in many ways you have fought on this journey alongside me, and for that I am grateful.

“I’ll always encourage you to continue the good fight, but you can’t fight it alone.”

After meeting on Twitter in 2014, things have been been plain sailing for the pair.

It was made public that Mike had cheated on Jana shortly after getting together.

However, Jana forgave the sports star and they got engaged at the end of 2014, and tied the knot in 2015.

Shortly after giving birth to daughter Jolie, the pair split again as Mike went into rehab, but they got back together a year later.

Three months after addressing Mike’s sex addiction, they welcomed son Jace into the world.

Just days before announcing their divorce Jana revealed plans to film a couple reality show with her husband.
